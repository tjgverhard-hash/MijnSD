What is the Diamond Casino Heist?

The Diamond Casino Heist is one of the most detailed and profitable heists in GTA Online. You plan the heist from the Arcade, choose an approach, complete prep missions, and then break into The Diamond Casino & Resort to empty the vault.

  • Playable with 2 to 4 players.
  • You choose 1 of 3 approaches: Silent & Sneaky, The Big Con, Aggressive.
  • Vault loot can be: Cash, Artwork, Gold, or Diamonds (diamonds are usually event-limited).

Important: exact payouts and availability of certain loot can change through updates and events.

Requirements and Start

  • You need an Arcade to use the heist planning board.
  • Talk to Lester to unlock the heist.
  • Do Casino Scope Out: find entry points, points of interest, and security details.
  • Then choose your approach and start prep missions.

Main Prep Categories

  • Approach-specific prep: outfits, route, tools, and entry plan.
  • Mandatory gear: hacking device, keycards, getaway vehicles, weapons.
  • Optional prep: Duggan Shipments, patrol routes, security intel.
  • Exit setup: disguises and a safe escape route.

Approach 1: Silent & Sneaky

  • Focus on stealth, camera control, and silent takedowns.
  • Good for teams with communication and timing.
  • Mistakes quickly trigger alarms, so route knowledge is crucial.
  • Often a strong choice for consistent, clean runs.

Approach 2: The Big Con

  • You enter with disguises and social engineering.
  • The right prep and disguises often make this the easiest route.
  • High control over pacing in the early phase.
  • Popular choice for fast money runs with an experienced team.

Approach 3: Aggressive

  • Direct action with heavy weapons and explosives.
  • Fewer stealth requirements, but more incoming damage and chaos.
  • Good for teams that prefer combat.
  • Higher risk of time loss and lower final payout because of damage/time.

Support Crew Explained

  • Gunman: determines loadout quality and survivability.
  • Driver: determines getaway vehicles and escape speed.
  • Hacker: determines time in the vault, often the most important choice.

The best value is usually a strong hacker, because extra vault seconds mean more loot directly.

Vault Loot and Payout (General Estimate)

Loot Value (approx.) Note
Cash Lowest payout Easy, but least profitable
Artwork Medium to high Faster to collect than cash/gold
Gold High Heavier, so planning and route are important
Diamonds Top payout Not always available, often event-only

Final payout is also affected by crew cuts, setup costs, and heist performance.

Must-Do Optional Preps

  • Duggan Shipments: greatly weakens casino security.
  • Level 2 Security Passes: makes movement in restricted areas easier.
  • Patrol Routes: better situational awareness in stealth.
  • Security Intel: helps plan entry/exit without surprises.
  • Exit Disguise: safer escape after the vault.

Best Practical Tips

  • Always pick a good hacker for more vault time.
  • Rotate approaches for better long-term payouts.
  • Do key prep missions first, then optional optimization.
  • Plan clear roles: hacker, crowd control, loot runner.
  • Use voice chat or clear callouts to avoid mistakes.

Common Mistakes

  • Shooting too early in stealth routes.
  • Choosing a poor hacker and then having too little vault time.
  • Skipping optional prep and then losing time in the finale.
  • Not agreeing on an exit route in advance.
  • Staying in combat too long instead of escaping cleanly.

Quick Endgame Checklist

  • Arcade active and setup board ready.
  • Scope Out complete: access points and points of interest found.
  • Approach chosen based on team skill.
  • Hacker prioritized in crew selection.
  • Main optional preps completed.
  • Entry, vault routine, and exit route agreed in advance.

With this foundation, you can run the Diamond Casino Heist consistently and profitably, even with different teams.
