Story of GTA 5

The story follows three criminals in Los Santos: Michael De Santa, a retired bank robber living under witness protection; Franklin Clinton, a young street hustler looking for bigger opportunities; and Trevor Philips, a violent and unpredictable criminal from Michael's past.

Michael returns to crime after financial trouble and meets Franklin, who becomes his partner. Their activities attract attention and eventually lead Trevor to discover that Michael is still alive, despite believing he had died years earlier during a failed heist. Trevor reunites with Michael, though their relationship is tense due to past betrayal.

The three begin working together, carrying out major heists and dangerous missions while being manipulated by corrupt government agents and powerful criminals. As they earn money and power, conflicts between them grow, especially as Trevor learns the truth about Michael's deal with the authorities.

In the end, after pulling off a massive gold heist, Franklin is forced to choose between killing Michael, killing Trevor, or risking everything to save them both. The story concludes based on this decision, with the "save both" option leading to the three eliminating their enemies and surviving together.
