What is the Cayo Perico Heist?

The Cayo Perico Heist is a standalone heist in GTA Online where you infiltrate El Rubio's private island. It is the most famous solo money-maker because you can play the finale alone and fully optimize the route yourself.

  • Playable solo or with a team.
  • Planning is done through the Kosatka (submarine).
  • Primary target + secondary loot determine your payout.

Note: payouts and loot spawns can change through GTA Online updates.

Requirements and Start

  • Buy the Kosatka to unlock the heist.
  • Complete the intel mission to scope the island.
  • Scout compound entry points and loot locations.
  • Then choose your vehicle, weapons, and finale approach.

Main Prep Missions

  • Approach vehicle (Longfin or Kosatka is popular).
  • Cutting Torch for silent entry points.
  • Fingerprint Cloner for compound hacks.
  • Weapon Loadout with suppressors for stealth.

Primary Targets

Target Value (approx.) Note
Sinsimito Tequila Low Most common primary target
Ruby Necklace Medium Great solo run target
Bearer Bonds High Good payout with a fast route
Pink Diamond Very high Top target for maximum profit
Panther Statue Event-only top payout Not always available

Your net profit depends on fencing fee, setup costs, and time during the finale.

Secondary Loot Priority

  • Cocaine: best value per bag space.
  • Weed: strong second option if cocaine is not available.
  • Paintings: easy and fast, especially solo inside the compound.
  • Cash: lowest priority, only take it as filler.

Best Stealth Route (Solo)

  • Start with Longfin or Kosatka, depending on your preference.
  • Grab secondary loot first if your route allows it.
  • Use the drainage tunnel for a safe compound entry.
  • Take the primary target, then exfil quickly via water.
  • Avoid unnecessary fights to complete the elite challenge.

Elite Challenge Tips

  • Goal: finish the finale in under 15 minutes.
  • Stay stealthy for consistent runs.
  • Use a fixed route and learn guard patterns.
  • Practicing hacks outside the finale saves a lot of time.
  • Plan your escape before entering the compound.

Common Mistakes

  • Taking out too many guards without a reason.
  • Not using suppressors in a stealth plan.
  • Not testing your route and getting lost on the island.
  • Filling your bag with low-value loot while better options are available.

Quick Checklist

  • Intel complete and loot locations noted.
  • Cutting torch + cloner + suppressors prepared.
  • Approach vehicle chosen based on your route.
  • Escape plan ready for a fast finale.
